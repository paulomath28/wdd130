Overview

Purpose

My purpose is to get more clients and expand my business. I need a website to reach a much larger number of customers, because today many people from all over the world have access to the internet, allowing trillions of accesses to sites from all over the world, and with this website created, potential customers and others will be able to find my company contact and information.

Audience

My target customers are people interested in rafting activities, either individual or as a family. Their life situacion are customers with an age range of 8-40 years old, focused on customers with enough money to pay for rafting trips (basic salary or higher). They want to go out to rafting locations and practice this sport as individuals or as a family. Many potential clients have been searching every day for more information about the practice of rafting, becoming enchanted and looking for companies almost all over the world that can offer the best service in this practice, with the maximum safety possible, with experienced professionals, with guides who Get to know the places where you will practice rafting and other activities of my company. Our partners from almost all over the world are prepared to give you the best rafting experience you can have.
